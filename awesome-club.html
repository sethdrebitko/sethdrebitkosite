Support Awesome, Be Awesome

Like many creators the path to independence I'm planning involves a wide range of potential revenue methods. A corner stone to my plan is to rely directly on the support and good will of the community. To this end I have set up the Awesome Club! The biggest thing that your help through the Awesome Club does is to literally buy me time (and redbull) because my plan is to take frequent (as possible) mini sabbaticals to get bursts of work done at a time.

What you get from this are a couple of things.

  • The warm fuzzies for helping an indie dev
  • Copies of my game experiments
  • Steam Keys for any released games
  • Access to my project kanban boards and knowledge base
  • Access to our Awesome Minecraft Server (at the $9.99 or higher level)
